## Iteration 3 — Scheduling + Events + Operational Readiness
### Feature requests
1. **Future planning**
   - Employees can set meal participation for a future date (within a limited forward window).
   - Admin/Logistics can view headcount forecasts for upcoming dates.

2. **Event meals**
   - Admin/Logistics can create “Event Meals” (e.g., Event Dinner) with:
     - Date
     - Meal type
     - Optional note (why/what event)
   - Employees can opt in/out specifically for event meals if applicable.

3. **Auditability and accountability**
   - Admin/Logistics can see “who changed what and when” for participation entries.
   - Team Leads/Admin edits are identifiable (so corrections are traceable).

4. **Operational dashboards**
   - Logistics/Admin dashboard includes:
     - Daily headcount snapshot
     - Upcoming forecast snapshot
     - Special day indicators (holiday/office closed/celebration)

5. **Policy refinements**
   - Cutoff rules, role permissions, and any restrictions can be refined based on feedback from Iteration 1–2 usage.

6. **Monthly WFH usage summary (soft limit)**
   - The system shows WFH days used per employee for the current month.
   - The standard allowance is 5 WFH days per month, but entries beyond the allowance are still accepted.

7. **Over-limit indicators in reports**
   - Team Lead and Admin/Logistics views clearly highlight employees who exceed the monthly allowance.
   - Reports include rollups such as: number of employees over limit and total extra WFH days.

8. **Over-limit filters**
   - Team Lead and Admin/Logistics views can filter to show only employees who are over the monthly allowance.
